Registration

Registering to vote can be quite a confusing process! You may be asking: How do I register to vote? How do I even know if I'm registered? And if I am registered, how do I find when and where to vote? We've compiled resources from the Illinois state government page just to help you out.

Follow one of the three paths below to go through the voter registration process!

How do I know if I'm registered?

This resource will ask you to enter in your personal information, and use it to determine if you are currently registered to vote in Illinois.

How do I register to vote?

If you know that you aren't currently registerd to vote, you can use the online application form provided by the Illinois government to register to vote!

Where do I go once I'm registered?

Once you're registered, you can use this resource to locate all of the nearby polling places near you, so you can go there and vote!
