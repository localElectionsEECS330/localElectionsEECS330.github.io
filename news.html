News and Education

One of the highest barriers of entry to participation in local government is the information gap. Government policy, although important, can sound excessively bureaucratic or technical for "everyday" people to understand. However, the brevity of social media can actually be helpful in navigating that barrier of entry! We've categorized Twitter feeds from pertinent government sources to help you contextualize and understand local politics better.
